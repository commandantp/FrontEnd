Ctrl+P Privacy Policy

Ctrl+P (“We”) is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. By visiting ctrl-p.co (“Our Site”) you are accepting and consenting to the practices described in this policy.

We may collect and process the following data about you: Information you give us. You may give us information about you by registering for our service or on our social media pages or profiles, or by corresponding with us by phone, e-mail or otherwise. Information we collect about you. With regard to each of your visits to our site we may automatically collect the following information: usage information, e.g. number of pages you have printed; technical information, e.g. about your browser or the device you use to access the site etc.; information about your visit, e.g. which website you clicked through to reach ours, lengths of visits to pages etc. Information we receive from other sources. We work closely with third parties (including, for example, business partners and subcontractors in technical, payment and delivery services) and may receive information about you from them.

COOKIES

Our website uses cookies to distinguish you from other users of our website. See also our Cookie Policy.

USES MADE OF THE INFORMATION

We use information held about you in the following ways: Information you give to us. Our primary purpose in collecting personal information is to provide you with a secure, smooth, efficient, and customized experience, while also allowing advertisers to reach you as a Ctrl+P user. When we deliver ads, we do not share your information (information that personally identifies you, such as your name or contact information) with advertisers unless you give us permission. We may provide advertisers with information when we have removed your name and other personally identifying information from it, or combined it with other information so that it no longer personally identifies you. For example, we may tell an advertiser how its ads perform or how many people viewed their ads. So we can show you content that you may find interesting, we may use all of the information we receive about you to serve ads that are more relevant to you. For many ads we serve, advertisers may choose their audience by location, demographics and any other information we receive or infer about users. We may also use your personal information to provide Ctrl+P services and customer support; process transactions and send notices about your transactions; resolve disputes, collect fees, and troubleshoot problems; prevent potentially prohibited or illegal activities, and enforce our User Agreement; customize, measure, and improve Ctrl+P services and the content, layout, and operation of our website and applications; deliver targeted marketing, service update notices, and promotional offers; contact you, as authorized by our User Agreement; compare information for accuracy and verify it with third parties; Information we collect about you. We will use this information: to administer, operate and improve our site and ensure it is presented in the most effective manner; as part of our efforts to keep our site safe and secure; to make suggestions and recommendations to you and other users of our site about goods or services that may interest you or them. Information we receive from other sources. We may combine this information with information you give to us and information we collect about you and use it for the purposes set out above.

DISCLOSURE OF YOUR INFORMATION

We may share your personal information with any member of our group and you should only give us your information if you consent to this.
